CHANGE 3 — DIFFERENTIATE أذكار vs أدعية
════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Update the display names for all 20 categories in ALL 
15 languages in i18n.ts using the correct Islamic 
terminology — أذكار for remembrance/recitation-based 
categories, أدعية for supplication-based categories:

CATEGORIES THAT ARE أذكار (remembrance/recitation):
  morning        → أذكار الصباح
  evening        → أذكار المساء
  after_prayer   → أذكار بعد الصلاة
  waking         → أذكار الاستيقاظ
  sleep          → أذكار النوم
  friday         → أذكار الجمعة
  ruqyah         → أذكار الرقية الشرعية
  istighfar      → أذكار الاستغفار  
                   (istighfar is repeated dhikr, not a one-off dua)

CATEGORIES THAT ARE أدعية (supplication):
  anxiety        → أدعية الهم والحزن
  distress       → أدعية الكرب
  quranic        → الأدعية القرآنية  (already correct)
  parents        → أدعية الوالدين
  rizq           → أدعية الرزق
  debt           → أدعية قضاء الدين
  faraj          → أدعية الفرج
  calamity       → أدعية رفع البلاء
  travel         → أدعية السفر
  visiting_sick  → أدعية عيادة المريض

SINGULAR (neither fits neatly as plural):
  istikhara      → دعاء الاستخارة  (singular — one specific dua)
  quran_khatm    → دعاء ختم القرآن (singular — one specific dua)

Update the English equivalents accordingly:
  أذكار → "Adhkar of..."   e.g. "Morning Adhkar"
  أدعية → "Duas of..."     e.g. "Duas for Anxiety"
  دعاء  → "Dua of..."      e.g. "Dua of Istikhara"

Full English values:
  morning:       "Morning Adhkar"
  evening:       "Evening Adhkar"
  after_prayer:  "Adhkar After Prayer"
  waking:        "Adhkar Upon Waking"
  sleep:         "Sleep Adhkar"
  friday:        "Friday Adhkar"
  ruqyah:        "Ruqyah Adhkar"
  istighfar:     "Adhkar of Istighfar"
  anxiety:       "Duas for Anxiety & Grief"
  distress:      "Duas for Distress"
  quranic:       "Quranic Duas"
  parents:       "Duas for Parents"
  rizq:          "Duas for Sustenance"
  debt:          "Duas for Debt Relief"
  faraj:         "Duas for Relief"
  calamity:      "Duas to Lift Hardship"
  istikhara:     "Dua of Istikhara"
  travel:        "Duas for Travel"
  visiting_sick: "Duas for the Sick"
  quran_khatm:   "Dua After Quran"

Apply equivalent terminological distinction accurately 
in all other 13 languages — أذكار/أدعية distinction 
exists in all languages that have borrowed these terms 
(Urdu, Indonesian, Malay, Persian, Turkish, Bengali).
For languages without direct equivalents use:
  fr: "Invocations" (adhkar) vs "Supplications" (duas)
