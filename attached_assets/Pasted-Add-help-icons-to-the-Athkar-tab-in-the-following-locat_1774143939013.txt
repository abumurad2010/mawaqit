Add help (ⓘ) icons to the Athkar tab in the following locations:

════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
LOCATIONS & CONTENT
════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

1. DISPLAY MODE TOGGLE (top of Athkar main screen)
   — small ⓘ to the right of the toggle bar
   — tapping shows:

   en: "Arabic: shows the dhikr text in Arabic only.\nTransliterated: adds Latin pronunciation and translation in your chosen language."
   ar: "عربي: يعرض النص العربي فقط.\nنقل حرفي: يضيف النطق اللاتيني والترجمة بلغتك المختارة."
   [all 15 languages]
   key: help_athkar_toggle

2. FAVOURITES — ⓘ next to the first favourited tile 
   (only shown the FIRST TIME a user has at least one 
   favourite — dismiss permanently via AsyncStorage 
   key 'athkar_fav_help_seen')
   — tapping shows:

   en: "Long-press any category to add or remove it from your favourites. Favourites appear first and are highlighted in gold."
   ar: "اضغط مطولاً على أي فئة لإضافتها إلى المفضلة أو إزالتها منها. تظهر المفضلة أولاً وتتميز باللون الذهبي."
   [all 15 languages]
   key: help_athkar_favourites

3. COUNTER — ⓘ in the dhikr reader screen header 
   (next to the reset button)
   — tapping shows:

   en: "Tap anywhere on a dhikr card to count. The card completes automatically when you reach the required number and advances to the next dhikr."
   ar: "اضغط في أي مكان على بطاقة الذكر للعدّ. تكتمل البطاقة تلقائياً عند الوصول إلى العدد المطلوب وتنتقل للذكر التالي."
   [all 15 languages]
   key: help_athkar_counter

4. LANGUAGE SELECTOR in transliteration mode
   — small ⓘ next to the language dropdown
   — tapping shows:

   en: "This changes the translation language for Athkar only. It does not affect the app's main language setting."
   ar: "يغيّر هذا لغة ترجمة الأذكار فقط، ولا يؤثر على لغة التطبيق الرئيسية."
   [all 15 languages]
   key: help_athkar_language

5. INDIVIDUAL DHIKR CARDS — for any dhikr that has 
   a note about correct performance (nafth, wiping body, 
   specific body position, etc.), add a small ⓘ badge 
   on the card itself. Tapping shows the relevant note 
   from the translation text.
   
   Specifically apply to these translationKeys:
   - athkar_sleep_ikhlas    (nafth + wiping instruction)
   - athkar_sleep_falaq     (nafth + wiping instruction)
   - athkar_sleep_nas       (nafth + wiping instruction)
   - athkar_sleep_aslamt    (make it last thing you say)
   - athkar_sleep_janb      (lie on right side instruction)
   - athkar_sick_3          (place hand on pain instruction)
   - athkar_sick_4          (said 7× to the sick person)
   - athkar_istikhara_1     (pray 2 rakat first instruction)
   - athkar_friday_3        (recite full surah instruction)
   - athkar_ruqyah_1        (blow into palms instruction)
