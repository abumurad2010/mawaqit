### Full Release Notes (English)
*Use for the store listing long description, press release, or your own records.*

---

#### 🌒 Crescent Sighting Lookup (New Feature)

A new crescent sighting tool appears below the calendar. Unlike a standard "new moon" finder that shows the invisible astronomical conjunction, this tool is built for **Islamic lunar month determination (ruʾyat al-hilāl)**:

- Shows the **expected date of first crescent visibility** (the evening after conjunction)
- Displays the **observability window** — the exact sunset-to-moonset interval when the crescent can be seen, computed from your location
- Month-by-month navigation to plan ahead for Ramadan, Eid, and Dhul Hijjah
- Astronomical conjunction time shown as a secondary reference
- A **help button** (?) explains in all 15 app languages why this tool differs from a standard astronomical new moon calculator

---

#### 🌙 Moon Phases on the Calendar

- Every day in the calendar grid now shows a live **moon phase emoji** (🌑🌒🌓🌔🌕🌖🌗🌘)
- Tap any day to open a detailed popup showing: phase name (in your language), illumination %, Hijri day, and — on crescent days — the **crescent observability window** (sunset → moonset)
- New moon (conjunction) days show the **exact local time** of the astronomical new moon
- All phase names correctly use Islamic observational terminology

---

#### 🕌 Eid Prayer — Smart Visibility & Configurable Time

- The **Eid Prayer row** now appears automatically on the main prayer screen and in Settings only when it is relevant:
  - **Eid al-Fitr**: visible from 29 Ramadan through 1 Shawwal
  - **Eid al-Adha**: visible from 8–10 Dhul Hijjah
- Your **Hijri adjustment** setting is respected — users who observe a day early or late will see the row appear on the correct day for their country
- Set your **local mosque's official Eid prayer time** using the scroll-wheel time picker — no more fixed default
- A help button explains when the row appears and why

---

#### 🤲 Dhuha & Qiyam / Tahajjud — Customizable Nafl Prayers

- Toggle **Dhuha** and **Qiyam (Tahajjud)** display on or off from Settings
- Set an exact **reminder time** for each using a smooth scroll-wheel time picker
- Both appear on the main prayer screen between the five daily prayers when enabled
- Reminders are scheduled at your configured times

---

#### ⏰ First Adhan Reminder — Finer Intervals

- Early adhan reminder intervals updated from coarse 10-minute steps to **5-minute steps**: Off, 5, 10, 15, 20, 25, 30 minutes
- Picker redesigned as a compact modal — takes less space in Settings

---

#### 🎨 Gold Accessibility Theme (8th Theme)

- A new **Gold** color theme joins the existing 7 accessibility themes
- Rich gold palette in both light mode (`#8B6800`) and dark mode (`#FFD60A`)
- Accessible contrast ratios maintained throughout

---

#### 🏠 Always Opens on Prayer Times Tab

- The app now always returns to the **Prayer Times** (home) tab when launched or resumed from background — no more landing on a different tab after switching away

---

#### 🛠 Settings — Compact & Cleaner

- All Settings rows now use **Inter** (sans-serif) for a consistent, modern feel
- Dhuha, Qiyam, and Maghrib rows collapsed into compact single-line layouts
- Accessibility theme tiles redesigned as a **2-column grid** — easier to scan
- All roller (scroll-wheel) modals close by tapping outside the sheet
